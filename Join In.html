Under 5's session on Saturday 20th October, 10.00am

We are running another under 5's session this month and aim to continuously run one of these sessions per month right through to the end of the season. If you are interested in bringing your child down to have a go at skiing then these sessions are ideal. We now have very limited availability for these sessions so please contact Mel on 0151 645 2452 to confirm your interest and to get more details.

Club Holiday 2012-2013

The location of this years Oval Ski Club holiday will be in either in St.Christoph/St.Anton in Austria. The holiday date is Saturday 23rd March - 30th March 2013. An update will be posted in the near future stating where this seasons holiday will be located. Group sizes will vary and if you want to go, ensure your book your place on the holiday before it is too late. Contact Andy Kearney on 0785 566 2891 for further details or to book your place.
